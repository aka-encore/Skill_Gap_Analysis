Frequently Asked Questions

How does Skill Gap Analysis work?

SkillBridge analyzes your performance across assessments and compares your scores against industry standards to highlight your strong concepts and vulnerabilities.

What happens if I switch tabs during an assessment?

Our automatic proctoring system flags tab switches. Accumulating 3 warnings triggers automatic test submission.

How do I claim my Certificate of Skill Attainment?

Achieve a score of 80% or higher on any assessment to unlock your verifiable digital certificate on the results screen.
